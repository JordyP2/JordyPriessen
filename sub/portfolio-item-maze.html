Perfect Maze

Randomly generated

For this project, I decided to create a Unity application that would be capable of randomly generating perfect maze, using a depth searh approach. This means the generator will have 1 path that will keep extending on until it reaches points where it cannot find unused neighboring tiles anymore. Then it tracks back to tiles that still have available neighboring tiles until every tile in the grid has become visited. The maze needs to be perfect! That means it follows the following principles:

  • The maze cannot have loops.
  • The maze cannot have closed circuits.
  • The maze cannot have innaccessible areas.
  • any two points must be connected through a singular, unbroken path.
