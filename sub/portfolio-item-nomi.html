Stichting NOMI

A fun way to NOMI

Stichting NOMI is a foundation that is committed to improve the lives of children that miss out on a normal life, for the reason that they are required to visit the hospital regularly due to their chronical illness.

NOMI attempts to reach this goal are by offering these children solutions such as interactive applications to find and interact with children with the same fate.

My addition to achieving this goal was to work with a team of programmers and Game Artists on a Game for the Mobile Phone that uses Augmented reality to take the player on a scavenger hunt within a certain location, which is usually a hospital. The scavenger hunt can be played by scanning signature NOMI branded images, each of which provide the player with a hint on what to do next. Some images then come with a minigame, which you need to complete to be able to either move on or complete the scavenger hunt.
