BonoTraffics

Driving simulator

BonoTraffics B.V. is a traffic advisor, providing several regions around the Netherland with their expertise about how to manage the dutch traffic.

My role to play a part of realising this was to create a driving simulator that allows them to easily express their advices in an easy to understand environment. As such, this driving simulator allows BonoTraffics to create realistic traffic scenarios with real traffic, anticipating towards traffic lights, signs and eachother. these scenarios can be created within the Unity editor itself, and can then be viewed through multiple different ways. such as:

  • Driving through the scenario with driving simulator hardware such as a steering wheel and pedals. Either on a screen or through VR.
  • Walking through the scenario through VR.
  • Observing through a bird views perspective with an extrernal screen.
